Who we are.

Thunai Associates LLP is a multi-disciplinary consultancy practice based in Ariyalur, Tamil Nadu. We bring together expertise in public policy, direct and indirect taxation, corporate law, and business advisory under a single integrated practice.

Our name — Thunai — means companion or support in Tamil. It reflects our belief that the best professional relationships are built on trust, continuity, and a genuine understanding of the challenges our clients face.
