Policy

Public Policy Advisory

We assist government bodies, civil society organisations, academic institutions, think tanks, and businesses in understanding, engaging with, and shaping public policy. Our work spans policy research, regulatory analysis, stakeholder engagement strategy, and evidence-based advocacy. We combine legal and economic analysis with an understanding of the Indian regulatory environment to deliver counsel that is both rigorous and actionable.

  • Policy research, analysis, and position papers
  • Regulatory impact assessments and compliance mapping
  • Stakeholder submissions and consultation responses
  • Government liaison and advocacy strategy support
  • Legislative and regulatory drafting assistance
  • Public sector compliance advisory and guidance
  • Inter-institutional and cross-sector policy coordination
Tax

Direct Tax

Our direct tax practice covers the full spectrum of income tax compliance and advisory for individuals, partnerships, companies, and trusts. We combine meticulous compliance work with proactive planning to minimise tax exposure within the bounds of law, and provide representation before tax authorities when required.

  • Income tax return filing for individuals, firms, and companies
  • Tax audits under the Income Tax Act
  • Advance tax computation and planning
  • TDS and TCS compliance, reconciliation, and return filing
  • Representation in assessments, scrutiny proceedings, and appeals
  • Responses to demand notices and departmental correspondence
  • Long-term tax structuring and business succession planning
Tax

Indirect Tax & GST

Our indirect tax practice focuses on Goods and Services Tax compliance, advisory, and dispute resolution. We help businesses stay current with evolving GST rules, optimise their tax positions, and respond effectively to departmental inquiries and notices.

  • GST registration, migration, and amendments
  • Monthly and quarterly return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, GSTR-9C)
  • GST audit, reconciliation, and annual return support
  • Input tax credit review and optimisation
  • Responses to show cause notices and departmental proceedings
  • GST refund claims (exports, inverted duty structure)
  • Sector-specific GST advisory and transaction structuring
Legal

Corporate & Commercial Legal

Our legal practice covers corporate structuring, commercial documentation, and transactional support. We draft and review contracts with an eye for commercial practicality and legal precision, helping businesses operate with clarity and reduce exposure to dispute.

  • Company incorporation, LLP formation, and structuring advice
  • Shareholders agreements, partnership deeds, and joint venture agreements
  • Vendor, customer, and distribution contracts
  • Employment agreements, consultancy contracts, and HR policies
  • Non-disclosure and confidentiality deeds
  • Legal opinions, certificates, and compliance declarations
  • Transaction support for mergers, acquisitions, and restructurings
Compliance

Compliance & Regulatory

We help businesses navigate the dense compliance obligations arising from corporate law, labour law, foreign exchange regulation, and sector-specific statutes. Our compliance practice ensures that entities remain in good standing with regulators and avoid the costs of non-compliance.

  • ROC annual filings and event-based compliance under the Companies Act
  • FEMA and RBI compliance for inbound and outbound transactions
  • MSME registration, Udyam certification, and related compliance
  • Professional tax, ESI, and PF registration and ongoing filings
  • Secretarial services and maintenance of statutory registers
  • Compliance health checks and gap assessments
Advisory

Business & Strategic Advisory

Beyond compliance, we support founders, directors, and institutions in making well-informed business decisions. Our advisory work draws on our combined tax and legal expertise to provide integrated perspective at critical inflection points.

  • Business structuring and entity selection advice
  • Due diligence support for investments and acquisitions
  • Start-up advisory on regulatory, tax, and legal matters
  • Family business succession and governance planning
  • Financial modelling and projections support
  • Institutional advisory for NGOs, trusts, and section 8 companies
